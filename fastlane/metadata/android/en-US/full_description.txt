WakeIQ wakes you gently by exploiting a well-documented property of human sleep:
your brain's sensitivity to sound varies dramatically with sleep stage. During deep
slow-wave sleep (N3), auditory arousal thresholds are high - quiet sounds go completely
unnoticed. During light sleep (N1, N2) and REM, thresholds are significantly lower and
moderate sound is sufficient to rouse you. WakeIQ's gradual ramp starts below the
threshold of any sleep stage and rises slowly. It passes unnoticed through deep sleep
and reaches you naturally when you surface into a lighter phase.

This is grounded in peer-reviewed science: Williams et al. (1964), Rechtschaffen et al.
(1966), and replicated consistently since. Waking from deep sleep causes sleep inertia -
fMRI studies show a 41% reduction in cognitive performance vs waking from light sleep
(Vallat et al. 2019; Hilditch and McHill 2019). WakeIQ addresses the cause. Full
citations are in the README at https://github.com/rajeshsub/WakeIQ

The bundled sounds are melodic and natural by design. A 2020 PLoS ONE study (McFarlane
et al., doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0215788) found melodic alarm sounds reduce perceived
sleep inertia compared to neutral tones.

HOW IT WORKS

Set a wake time and smart window (default 20 min, configurable 10-30 min). WakeIQ
starts a sound ramp at a barely audible level and raises it slowly over a configurable
duration (5-30 min). During deep sleep it goes unnoticed. When you cycle into a lighter
stage your arousal threshold drops and the sound wakes you smoothly. If you stay in
deep sleep through the window, the ramp reaches full volume at your hard deadline. You
always wake on time.

Paired with a Wear OS watch: wrist accelerometer data can trigger the ramp slightly
earlier if light-sleep movement is detected, giving you an even softer start. This is
an optional enhancement. The ramp works fully without a watch.

SOUNDS

Custom audio from your device is also supported. Snooze is included.

PRIVACY

WakeIQ collects no personal data of any kind. No analytics. No crash telemetry. No
sensor data leaves your device. No remote servers. No accounts. Nothing is ever
transmitted anywhere. Your sleep patterns, alarm schedule, and habits are yours alone.
There is no data to sell because none is collected.
