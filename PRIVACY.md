# Privacy Policy for WakeIQ

**Effective date:** 18 June 2026
**Last updated:** 18 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the WakeIQ Android application ("WakeIQ", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles information. It applies to the WakeIQ app distributed through the Google Play Store and as open-source software.

## The short version

**WakeIQ does not collect, store off your device, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.** There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no remote servers. Everything the app does happens locally on your device. We genuinely have no way to see your alarms, your settings, your sleep patterns, or anything else about you.

WakeIQ is also fully open source. You do not have to take our word for any of this; you can read the complete source code at **https://github.com/rajeshsub/WakeIQ**.

## Information we collect

**None.** WakeIQ does not collect any personal data or sensitive user data, including but not limited to:

- No name, email address, phone number, or contact details
- No account or login of any kind
- No location data
- No advertising identifiers or device identifiers
- No usage analytics, telemetry, or behavioural data
- No crash or diagnostic reports sent to us
- No sleep data, motion data, or alarm history transmitted anywhere

The app does not contain any advertising and does not display ads of any kind.

## Information stored on your device

To function as an alarm clock, WakeIQ stores the following **locally on your device only**, never transmitted to us or any third party:

- Your alarms (times, days, labels, colours, sound choices, and settings)
- Your app preferences (such as ramp duration, snooze length, smart-wake window, and theme)

This information is held in the app's private storage on your device. It is removed when you delete the app or clear the app's data through your device settings. We have no access to it at any time.

## Permissions and why they are used

WakeIQ requests certain Android permissions purely to operate as a reliable alarm clock. None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data:

| Permission | Purpose | Data leaves device? |
|---|---|---|
| Exact alarm scheduling | Fire alarms at the precise time you set | No |
| Notifications | Show the alarm and its controls | No |
| Foreground service | Keep the alarm playing so the system does not kill it | No |
| Wake lock / full-screen intent / keyguard | Wake the screen and show the alarm over the lock screen | No |
| Boot completed | Re-register your alarms after the device restarts | No |
| Read audio / external storage | Let you choose your own audio file as an alarm sound, read locally only | No |
| Motion / high-rate sensors | Optional on-device motion detection to refine wake timing | No |
| Write settings | Adjust screen brightness during the wake ramp | No |
| Vibrate | Vibrate during an alarm | No |
| Ignore battery optimisations | Ask the system not to delay alarms in order to save battery | No |

If you choose a custom sound file, WakeIQ reads that file from your device only to play it as your alarm. The file is never copied, uploaded, or shared.

## Third parties

WakeIQ contains **no third-party advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking SDKs**. We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any.

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may independently collect certain information (for example, aggregated, anonymised crash and performance diagnostics through Android and Google Play, or installation statistics) in accordance with **Google's own Privacy Policy** (https://policies.google.com/privacy). This collection is performed by Google at the platform level, is governed by Google's policies rather than ours, and is not something WakeIQ controls, initiates, or receives identifiable data from.

## Children's privacy

WakeIQ is a general-purpose alarm clock and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children.

## Data security

Because WakeIQ stores data only on your device and transmits nothing, your information is protected by your device's own security (such as your screen lock and the operating system's app sandboxing). There is no server, database, or account that could be breached on our side, because none exists.

## Your rights

Privacy regulations such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Australian Privacy Principles grant rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. Because WakeIQ holds no personal data about you on any server, there is nothing for us to access, export, correct, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of the data stored locally on your device and can remove it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes to the app or to legal requirements. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Because the app is open source, the full history of changes to this policy is also visible in the project's version-control history.
